Today, Xiaobian will take you to quickly understand the key strategies and basic common sense of car maintenance projects in 3 minutes!

Car maintenance tips: 5 must-know key items

In the daily use of the car, there are 5 key points to pay special attention to, hurry up and note it! Warm up the car before driving: After starting the car, wait about 30 seconds for the car to warm up and allow the oil and engine to idle before driving. Avoid frequent driving for short distances: long distance driving is conducive to battery recharging and carbon removal, often driving short distances , or driving a car for a long time, not only can not achieve the above effects, but is easy to hurt the car. Avoid long-term exposure to the sun: avoid exposing the vehicle to the sun for a long time, which is easy to accelerate the aging of the interior; It should also be avoided to park in places with a large amount of fallen leaves and bird droppings, which can easily cause damage to the vehicle's air intake system. Avoid washing the car under the scorching sun: Washing the car under the scorching sun can easily cause the paint to lose its luster, in addition, waxing should be carried out in a straight line, avoiding waxing in a circle, which is easy to cause a concentric circle halo on the surface of the paint. Maintenance according to time & mileage: The car should be maintained at the factory according to the specified time or mileage, if there is a problem with the car, it can also be found as soon as possible in the maintenance process and overhauled as soon as possible.

The correct maintenance cycle of the car, according to the mileage or time into the factory maintenance?

There are many types of car maintenance items, and the most basic regular maintenance items are the "five oils and three waters" that are known to motorists. Car owners can check the condition of five oils and three waters by themselves before they are about to hit the road or after a long drive to avoid problems in the vehicle. The so-called five oils refer to transmission oil, engine oil, brake oil, power steering oil and gasoline. And the three waters refer to tank water, battery water, and wiper water. (Further reading: If you want to know more about what "five oils and three waters" are, please refer to this article - What are five oils and three waters?) To maintain your car, it is essential to check one) And how often should the five oils and three waters be replaced? When the owner is buying a new car, if the car manufacturer tells you that you need to go back to the original factory for maintenance if you drive 5,000 kilometers in half a year, but when the owner drives for half a year and only runs 2,000 kilometers, does it need to go to the factory for maintenance? In terms of safety, 5,000 kilometers or half a year must be maintained first, that is, when the mileage or time, one of the conditions is reached, it should be maintained in the factory once.

Even if you don't drive often, the opened engine oil will still oxidize and deteriorate after a long time, even if the mileage has not reached or the frequency of use of the car is not high, if it meets the replacement standards according to the time, it is still recommended to enter the factory for maintenance. For car owners with high frequency of use, car parts or accessories are easy to consume and age, so when the car reaches the mileage specified in the car maintenance, it must be maintained in the factory.
